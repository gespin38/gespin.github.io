leave behind | 3.25” × 4.75 × .5” | 2020

some people give out business cards….others give out boxes of crayons (with a piece of drawing paper). why crayons? one reason is because it brings back memories from when they would give me crayons at restaurants for me to draw on a placemat as a kid. the second reason is because each box contains one of my favorite color schemes—because as simple and repetitive as it sounds, i’ve always enjoyed everything about color. every box contains a different color scheme, in hopes that when you use them, you will enter a space of excitement and fun, the same way i feel when i start creating with these colors.
