dusty complications in the stratosphere

infographic | 16” × 24” | 2018

dusty complications in the stratosphere is an infographic focusing on 85 space dust particles collected from june 30th and july 30th, 2008 during 50 hours of flight time over the eastern coast of the usa, the great lakes, and canada. each of those 85 dust particles is shown in this chart along with their elemental composition to see if there is a specific element that is causing climate change and interference with our radio stations.
